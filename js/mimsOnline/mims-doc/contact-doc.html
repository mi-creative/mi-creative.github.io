Contact Module



@{name} contact @{m1} @{m2} {stiffness} {damping} {opt: thresh}

The contact interaction computes a viscoelastic collision between the masses {@m1} and {@m2} when the difference in position between both masses is lower than the given threshold distance.

Physical Parameters: stiffness, damping and (optional) threshold distance.

Example:
@g1 ground 0.
@m1 mass 1.2 0. 0.1
@c contact @g1 @m1 0.01 0.001 0.

Bounded Non-linear Spring Damper

@{name} nlSpringDamper @{m1} @{m2} {stiffness} {nl3stiffness} {stiffnessMax} {damping}
This non linear spring damper connects two material points {@m1} and {@m2} with a non linear viscoelastic interaction, using a cubic term. The equivalent stiffness term is clipped beyond the {stiffness_lim} parameter, meaning the interaction will revert to a linear behaviour in extreme ranges (rather than exploding!).
Linear stiffness, cubic stiffness, bounding stiffness and damping.
Example:
@g1 ground 0.
@m1 mass 1.2 0. 0.1
@sp nlSpringDamper @g1 @m1 0.01 0.0001 0.001

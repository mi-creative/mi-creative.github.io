Spring Damper Module



@{name} springDamper @{m1} @{m2} {stiffness} {damping}

The spring damper connects two material points {@m1} and {@m2} with a linear viscoelastic interaction.

Physical Parameters: stiffness and damping.

Example:
@g1 ground 0.
@m1 mass 1.2 0. 0.1
@sp springDamper @g1 @m1 0.01 0.001
